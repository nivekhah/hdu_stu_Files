Sample behavioral waveforms for design file "CNT11B.v"

The following waveforms show the behavior of lpm_counter megafunction for the chosen set of parameters in design "CNT11B.v". The design "CNT11B.v" is a 11 bit up counter. The counter supports a synchronous load of data input.

Fig. 1 : Wave showing counter operation.

The output port cout will be asserted at the completion of count sequence. The ports cin and cout are used to chain multiple counters together.
